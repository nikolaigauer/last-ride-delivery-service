**Fragile hanging bridges** — one-off bridges that collapse after the hearse crosses. Single-use, creates tension. Good for later chapters.

**Plank mechanic** — player gets out and moves planks lying on the ground to flatten rough terrain sections. Inspired by the difficulty of steep Matter.js terrain. Low-tech solution that fits the noir tone.

**Winch** — considered but ruled out. Directly inspired by "Are We There Yet" (camper game with front/back winch). Decided a hearse wouldn't have a winch, and it doesn't fit the tone. Don't revisit unless tone shifts.

**Why:** All noted during bridge planning session 2026-05-15.

**How to apply:** Fragile bridges and plank mechanic are viable for later chapters. Reference these when designing chapter 2+ puzzles or terrain obstacles.
